Chicken

Genetic studies have pointed to multiple origin in South Asia, Southeast Asia and East Asia but with the clade found in the America, Europe and Africa originating in the Indian subcontinent. From ancient India, the domesticated chicken spread to Lydia in western Asia Minor. (Back to Top)

Beef

Most beef skeletal muscle meat can be used as is by merely cutting into certain parts, such as roasts, short ribs or steak , while other cuts are processed (corned beef or beef jerky). Trimmings, on the other hand, are usually mixed with meat from older, leaner cattle, are ground, minced or used in sausages. (Back to Top)

Sushi

Sushi is traditionally made with medium-grain white rice, though it can be prepared with brown rice or short-grain rice. It is very often prepared with seafood, such as squid, eel, yellowtail, salmon, tuna or imitation crab meat. Many types of sushi are vegetarian. (Back to Top)
